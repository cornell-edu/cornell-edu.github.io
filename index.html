Frequently Asked Questions

Is Big Red Beds managed by Cornell Housing or affiliated with Cornell University?

No, Big Red Beds is a student-run operation.

Please do not reach out to Cornell Housing with questions regarding Big Red Beds — all inquiries should be sent to team@bigredbeds.com. Thank you for your understanding!

What happens to the university's smaller bed?

The university mattress is stored underneath your Big Red Bed, stood vertically against the wall. Cornell policy dictates that students cannot remove university furniture from the room, so this is where the additional under-bed storage space comes in handy.

As an added bonus, the old mattress is perfect to bring out for your visitors from out of town!

Where do I sleep if my Big Red Bed will be installed a few days after I arrive?

If you ordered sheets or additional bedding products, you can pick them up before your installation date, so you can use them with the university supplied Twin XL until we deliver your Big Red Bed.

If you would like to a upgrade to an earlier installation date, please reach out to contact@bigredbeds.com or our Instagram.

Will a Big Red Bed fall off or break?

Our Big Red Beds are installed on our secure frame extender attached to the original bed frame. You don't ever have to worry about the bed frame sliding off.

Check out our Instagram reels! We made a video showing that, even with four people jumping onto the edge of the bed, it won't break or flip. Pretty neat!

What if I don't know my dorm address yet?

No worries — you don't need to know yet. We'll reach out to coordinate where to install your Big Red Bed.

Do I get to keep my sheets, pillows, etc?

The sheets, pillow cases, pillows, blankets, etc. that may come with your bundle are yours to keep! (Psst, our 4-piece brushed microfiber sheet sets have a retail value of $60!) The only items we take back are the bed and frame extenders, saving you the trouble of storage.

Are Big Red Beds fire code compliant?

All of our Big Red Beds have passed fire inspections by both city and school inspectors. Our mattresses are certified fire-resistant, too. But you might still get compliments saying that your bed is "fire"… 🔥

How often are Big Red Beds replaced? Are they clean?

Big Red Beds are replaced every 4 years (we recycle them!), are always used with waterproof covers that you get to keep. In comparison, at Cornell, we've heard reports of dorm beds that are really, really old.

Thankfully, YOUR Big Red Bed will be brand, brand new. 2024-2025 marks our first full year in business, and because all of our existing customers are renting again, new customers will likely receive new beds!
